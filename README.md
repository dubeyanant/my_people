# My People

My People is a personal journaling app designed to help you capture and reflect on interactions, memories, and the little details about the people who matter most to you. It provides a dedicated space to document your relationships with friends, family, colleagues, or everyday acquaintances, helping you build deeper connections by recalling exactly what counts.

## ✨ Key Features

- **Dedicated Profiles**: Create a personal profile for everyone important in your life.
- **AI Persona Chat**: Interact with an AI persona of your contacts to get summaries of past interactions, brainstorm gift ideas, or explore conversational scenarios.
- **Visual Memories**: Add profile photos to bring faces and memories to the forefront.
- **Journal Entries**: Document past conversations, shared moments, and personal reflections in a clean, diary-style format.
- **Custom Notes & Milestones**: Track unlimited custom notes—from interests and life updates to milestones and funny stories.
- **Quick Search**: Instantly revisit specific entries or memories.
- **Modern UI/UX**: Enjoy a clean, intuitive, and distraction-free interface.
- **Privacy First**: Everything stays strictly local on your device. No cloud storage is required (except for AI chat processes).

## 💡 Perfect For

- Reflecting on past meetups and shared experiences.
- Keeping track of birthdays, anniversaries, and special occasions.
- Observing how your relationships evolve over time.
- Preparing thoughtful follow-ups, conversation starters, or gift ideas.
- Maintaining a personal, private record of important life events and conversations.
